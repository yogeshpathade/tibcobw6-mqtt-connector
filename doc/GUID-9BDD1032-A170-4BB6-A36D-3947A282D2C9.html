Uninstalling the Plug-in

You can uninstall the plug-in from the Eclipse Update Manager.

Procedure

  1. Open TIBCO Business Studio in one of the following ways:
    • Microsoft Windows: click Start > All Programs > TIBCO > TIBCO Business Studio version_number > Studio for Designers.
    • Linux/Mac OS: run the TIBCO Business Studio executable located in the TIBCO_HOME/studio/version_number/eclipse directory.
  2. From the menu, click Help > Install New Software to open the Eclipse Update Manager.
  3. In the Available Software dialog, click the link.
  4. In the Installed Software tab, select the product to be uninstalled and click Uninstall.
  5. In the Uninstall Details dialog, review the product to be uninstalled. Click Finish.
  6. Click Restart Now when prompt to restart TIBCO Business Studio.
